# Licensing Transition

Netbrowse Voice is moving its public Community Edition source to
GPL-3.0-or-later.

## Scope

The GNU GPL v3 or later applies to the default-branch source and any release
archive that includes the GPL [LICENSE](LICENSE) and the associated Community
Edition notices.

## Earlier releases

Earlier releases that were published with a different licence, including a
permissive Apache-2.0 notice, retain the rights already granted for those
copies. A licence change cannot revoke a permission that was already given to
recipients of an earlier release.

## Why this is recorded

This document prevents confusion between historical release terms and the
current Community Edition policy. It does not change third-party licences or
provider terms, and it is not legal advice.
